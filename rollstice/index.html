Do you want to fight dragons, steal spaceships or hunt down supervillains?
If that's the case, roleplaying is for you.
Come to Rollspelssällskapet and try it out!

When:

4-5 January, 10-22

Where:

Spelens Hus
Nobelvägen 145A, Malmö


During the weekend we will be offering a multitude of different games, from short drop-in games to campaigns that stretch the entire weekend.


The following games will all be available, with more to be announced:

Dungeons & Dragons 5th Edition

The world's biggest role-playing game offers an incredible world filled with dragons, magic and heroes.

Star Wars: Edge of the Empire

Explore the familiar galaxy from a new perspective, and with a fantastic narrative game system.

My Little Pony: Tails of Equestria

Adventure in this fantastical world of friendship and magic.

Masks: A New Generation

Don your superhero identity in this world of extraordinary individuals.

Deadlands: Reloaded

Visit the Wild West, with a supernatural twist.

(A more exact schedule will be released closer to the event.)
